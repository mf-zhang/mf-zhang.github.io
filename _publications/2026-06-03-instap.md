InstAP is an Instance-Aware Pre-training framework that jointly optimizes global vision-text alignment and fine-grained instance-level contrastive alignment by grounding textual mentions to specific spatial-temporal regions. It also presents InstVL, a large-scale dataset (2 million images, 50,000 videos) with dual-granularity annotations. InstAP outperforms existing VLP models on instance-level retrieval and achieves competitive zero-shot performance on video benchmarks including MSR-VTT and DiDeMo.
